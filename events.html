What Is HiveConnect?

HiveConnect is our annual showcase and networking event created to help students build confidence, share their work, and connect with people already working in the industry. It's a space where projects are celebrated, conversations happen naturally, and learning goes beyond the classroom. Through showcases, hands-on sessions, and honest conversations with professionals, students get the chance to be seen, heard, supported — and leave with clarity on what's next.

Upcoming Events

Beyond HiveConnect, The Hive hosts smaller events throughout the year focused on skill-building, career preparation, and community. These events are designed to be practical, welcoming, and easy to join — whether you're building confidence, refining your work, or meeting new people.
