Event FAQ

HiveConnect is an annual showcase and networking event where students present their projects, connect with industry professionals, and explore opportunities beyond the classroom. It's a full day of real conversations, hands-on sessions, and community building.

HiveConnect 2026 will be hosted on campus at the University of Lethbridge. The exact venue and room details will be shared with registered attendees closer to the event date.

Just yourself and a positive attitude! If you're showcasing a project, bring any materials or a laptop to present your work. Business cards or a LinkedIn QR code are great for networking. No formal dress code — come as you are.

HiveConnect is open to all University of Lethbridge students regardless of program, year, or experience level. Industry guests, mentors, and partners are also welcome to attend and connect with students.

Tickets are non-refundable once purchased. However, if you're unable to attend you're welcome to transfer your ticket to another student — reach out to us at thehive.club@uleth.ca to arrange the transfer.
