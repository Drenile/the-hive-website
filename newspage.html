LeadFest 2026: A Day Built Around Real Growth

January 26, 2026 · Event Recap · 5 min read
120+ Attendees
18 Industry Guests
8 Student Projects Showcased
6hrs Of Programming

What Happened

LeadFest 2026 brought together students, creatives, and industry professionals for a full day centered on growth, collaboration, and real-world skill building. Designed to bridge the gap between classroom learning and professional experience, this year's LeadFest reflected exactly what The Hive stands for — learning by doing, together.

From interactive workshops to student project showcases and honest one-on-one conversations with professionals, the day created space for students to explore their strengths, get real feedback, and leave with clarity on what comes next.

Highlights From The Day

The morning kicked off with a keynote from a local tech founder who shared their own non-linear path into the industry. The message was clear: the skills you build outside of class often matter more than the grades on your transcript.

🎤
Student Showcase

Eight Hive teams presented their semester projects to a panel of industry guests. Three teams received direct follow-up from companies interested in collaboration.

💬
Speed Mentoring Sessions

Students rotated through 10-minute one-on-one conversations with professionals across software, design, marketing, and business development.

🛠️
Hands-On Workshops

Three parallel workshop tracks ran throughout the afternoon — covering portfolio building, technical interview prep, and personal branding.

🤝
Open Networking

The day closed with an informal networking session. Students exchanged contacts, found collaborators, and several left with interview invitations in hand.

"I walked in not knowing what to expect. I left with three new connections, feedback on my portfolio, and honestly — a lot more confidence." — LeadFest 2026 Attendee

What's Next

LeadFest happens once a year, but the momentum it creates doesn't stop there. Several collaborations that started at LeadFest are now active Hive projects. If you missed this year's event, keep an eye on our events page — registration for LeadFest 2027 will open in the fall.

Want to get involved before then? Check out our upcoming events or reach out about joining a project team.
